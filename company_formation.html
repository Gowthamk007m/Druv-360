Company Formations

At DRUV360°, we understand that forming a company is a critical step in your entrepreneurial journey. Whether you're looking to establish a Private Limited Company (PVT LTD), Limited Liability Partnership (LLP), Partnership, or Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprise (MSME), our expert team is here to guide you through the process seamlessly and efficiently.
Private Limited Company (PVT LTD) Formation

A Private Limited Company is one of the most popular and widely used business structures. It offers limited liability protection, ease of raising funds, and credibility.

  • Legal Compliance: We handle all the legal requirements, including registration with the Registrar of Companies (ROC), drafting a Memorandum of Association (MOA) and Articles of Association (AOA), and obtaining necessary licenses.
  • Shareholding Structure: We assist in setting up the shareholding pattern, ensuring clarity in ownership and equity distribution.
  • Tax Benefits: Enjoy various tax advantages available to PVT LTD companies with our guidance on compliance and tax planning.
Limited Liability Partnership (LLP) Formation

An LLP combines the benefits of a partnership and a company, offering limited liability protection to its partners while maintaining the flexibility of a partnership.

  • Registration Process: We manage the entire registration process, including obtaining Digital Signature Certificates (DSC), Director Identification Numbers (DIN), and filing incorporation documents.
  • Partnership Agreement: Our experts draft a comprehensive LLP agreement outlining the roles, responsibilities, and profit-sharing ratios of the partners.
  • Regulatory Compliance: We ensure ongoing compliance with statutory requirements, including annual filings and financial disclosures.
Partnership Firm Formation

A Partnership Firm is a simple and flexible business structure suitable for small and medium-sized enterprises. It allows partners to share profits and responsibilities.

  • Partnership Deed: We draft a detailed partnership deed covering all aspects of the partnership, such as capital contributions, profit-sharing, and management duties.
  • Registration Assistance: While registration of a partnership firm is optional, we guide you through the process to enhance the credibility and legal standing of your business.
  • Tax Registration: We assist in obtaining necessary tax registrations, including PAN and GST, to ensure smooth business operations.
Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSME) Registration

MSMEs play a crucial role in the economy, and registering as an MSME provides various benefits, including subsidies, tax rebates, and easier access to credit.

  • Udyam Registration: We help you obtain Udyam Registration, which is mandatory for availing benefits under the MSME scheme.
  • Subsidies and Incentives: Our team informs you about various government subsidies and incentives available to MSMEs and assists in the application process.
  • Support Services: We offer ongoing support to ensure your MSME remains compliant with regulatory requirements and maximizes available benefits.
